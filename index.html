N R Nandhini is a dedicated QA Automation Engineer with over 5.5 years of hands-on experience ensuring software reliability, quality, and performance in the Banking industry. Currently employed at Cognizant Technology Solutions, she has been consistently engaged as Quality Analyst for the Royal Bank of Canada (RBC) — one of the world's largest financial institutions — across multiple platform projects.

Her expertise spans the full testing lifecycle: from requirements analysis and test case design through to execution, defect management, and release sign-off. She is skilled in building and maintaining Selenium-based automation frameworks in Java, developing complex SQL queries for backend data validation, and integrating test suites into CI/CD pipelines via Jenkins.

Nandhini holds a Master of Computer Applications (MCA) from Thiagarajar College of Engineering with a 8.93 CGPA, demonstrating strong academic grounding in computer science fundamentals. Her internship at Cognizant, where she trained in Core Java, SQL, Software Testing and Selenium, laid the foundation for her professional excellence in automation engineering.
